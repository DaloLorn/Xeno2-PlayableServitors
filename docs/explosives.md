# Explosives & Environmental Effects

## Environmental Effects

Gas DR resists all Stun effects.

- Thin Smoke: -10% ACC, 33% Dissipation, 2 Stun on Enter/Move, 10 Stun on EndInside
- Heavy Smoke: -20% ACC, 25% Dissipation, 5 Stun on Enter/Move, 20 Stun on EndInside
    - Stun Gas has 33% Dissipation and 10/40 Stun.
- Poison Gas: -20% ACC, 33% Dissipation, 2/5 Stun/Gas on Enter/Move, 10/20 Stun/Gas on EndInside
- Fire: 20% Dissipation (unless permanent), 100% Spread (unless static), 10/2/2 Energy/Morale/Suppression on Enter/Move, 25/1/10/10 Energy/Destruction/Morale/Suppression on EndInside
    - Energy DR resists all effects except Destruction (I think).
- Chemical Gas: -20% ACC, 35/5/1/5/5 Gas/Chemical/Destruction/Morale/Suppression on Enter/Move, 60/20/4/20/20 on EndInside
    - Gas DR affects 80% of Morale/Suppression.

## Frag Grenade

- 30 Kinetic, 10 Penetration, 5 Destruction, Blast 2.5, 5% Thin Smoke
- Alenium Grenade: 45 Kinetic, 15 Penetration, 8 Destruction
- Fusion Grenade: 75 Energy, 25 Penetration, 20 Destruction, 50% Fire

## Thermal Detonator

- Plasma Detonator: 50 Energy, 10 Penetration, 15 Destruction, Blast 2.5, 5% Thin Smoke, 25% Fire
- Fusion Detonator: 75 Energy, 25 Penetration, 50% Fire

## Symbiote Egg

- 5 Kinetic, Blast 1, 100% Spawn Symbiote, unusable by Xenonaut units
- Symbiote Pod: 100% Poison Gas

## Gas Detonator

- Toxic Detonator: 5 Kinetic, Blast 2.5, 100% Poison Gas, unusable by Xenonaut units
- Chemical Detonaor: 15 Chemical, replace Poison with Corrosive Gas

## Gas Grenade

- Smoke Grenade: 5 Kinetic, Blast 4, 100% Thick Smoke
- Stun Grenade: replace Thick Smoke with Stun Gas

## Flash Grenade

- Flashbang Grenade: 5 Kinetic, Blast 2.5
- Electroshock Grenade: 20 Shock, 40 Stun, 60 EMP, 50 Penetration

## Demolition Charge

- 25 Explosive, 20 Penetration, 5 Destruction, Blast 2.5, 10% Thin Smoke, 3% Fire
- Alenium Charge: 35 Explosive, 30 Penetration, 10 Destruction
- Fusion Charge: 50 Explosive, 40 Penetration, 25 Destruction, 75% Fire

## HEVY Launcher

All stats are relative to whichever grenade is loaded into the HEVY. All Xenonaut grenades (except charges) can be loaded into the HEVY.

- +1 Blast, 6 Ammo, Burst x3 (45% ACC, 65% TU), Weight 15/8
