# Melee Weapons

## Preface

Melee weapons are extremely lightweight, TU-efficient, resistant to overwatch, and devastating against unarmored units, but tend to suffer considerably against armor, with limited penetration and very little destruction.

Most melee weapons have the NeverGibs flag. Only Massive weapons such as the Power Fist do not.

Explosive damage is used as a stand-in for blunt force trauma, and accompanied by heavy stun damage.

Unless specified otherwise, melee attacks always have a 100% ACC modifier.

## Knives

- Combat Knife: 15 Kinetic, 40 Penetration, 10% TU, 2x REF, 100% ACC, 10% Bleed (x3), 4 Weight
- Vibroknife: 25 Kinetic, 60 Penetration, 4 Destruction, Bleed x5

## Batons

- Stun Baton: 5 Explosive (there is a shock, but not as damaging as the impact), 20 Stun, 30 EMP, 75 Penetration, 15% TU, 1.5x REF, 80% ACC, 6 Weight
- Electroshock Baton: 5 Shock, 30 Stun, 45 EMP, 125 Penetration

## Integrated

Integrated weapons prevent the wielder from equipping any other secondary weapons.

- Power Fist: 60 Explosive, 40 Stun, 75 Penetration, 25% TU, Massive 
- Mentarch Tentacles: 20 Explosive, 15 Stun, 60 Penetration, 15% TU
- Sebillian Claws: 40 Kinetic, 10 Stun, 40 Penetration, 2 Destruction, 20% TU
- Mantid Bite: 40 Kinetic, 20 Penetration, 1 Destruction, 15% TU

## Reaper

Reaper claws spawn zombies on kill.

- Reaper Claws: 40 Kinetic, 60 Penetration, 4 Destruction, 15% TU, 8.5% Bleed (x6!)
- Reaper Alpha Claws: 60 Kinetic, 80 Penetration, 6 Destruction, 10% Bleed

## Miscellaneous

- Zombie Fists: 10 Explosive, 10 Stun, 40 Penetration, 25% TU
- Symbiote Bite: 10 Kinetic, 80 Penetration, 4 Destruction, 10% TU, 3x REF, 50% Bleed
