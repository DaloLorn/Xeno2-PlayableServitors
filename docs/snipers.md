# Sniper Rifles

Sniper rifles do not receive short range bonuses, and therefore only have a LongRange.

## Ballistic (ancestor to all snipers)

- 40 Kinetic, 25 Suppression, 15 Penetration, 4 Destruction, 15% Bleed (x2)
- Size: 5x2
- Weight: 14
- Aimed Shot: 48% TU, 80% ACC (+20% if not moved)
- Scoped Shot: 64% TU, 160% ACC (+30%)
- Reload: 38 TU
- Range: 26 (10), max 36
- Ammo: 5 (weight 4)
- Accelerated: 45 Kinetic, 25 Penetration, 6 Destruction
- Advanced Accelerated: 50 Kinetic, 35 Penetration, 8 Destruction
- Alloy Rounds: Ammo Weight 3, +4 Penetration

## Laser Lance

- 12 Energy, 25 Penetration, 6 Destruction, 10 Suppression, 0% Bleed
- Range: 13 (4), max 38
- Ammo: 12 (regen 1)
- Aimed Shot: 100% ACC
- Scoped Shot: 190% ACC
- Alloy Optics: 17 Energy, 30 Penetration, 10 Destruction
- Supercapacitors: 18 Ammo, regen 2

## Plasma/Sniper

- 60 Energy, 15 Penetration, 40 Destruction, 85% Fire, 0% Bleed, 50 Suppression
- Micro-Fusion Chamber: 80 Energy, 50 Destruction, 15% Bleed (x3), 63 Suppression
- Atmospheric Feeds: Ammo Regen
- Range: 18 (12.5), max 26
- Ammo: 3

## Gauss

- 65 Kinetic, 40 Penetration, 8 Destruction, 15% Bleed (x2), 50 Suppression
- Supermagnetic Rails: 70 Kinetic, 50 Penetration, 12 Destruction, 63 Suppression
- Stiletto Rounds: +10 Penetration, +4 Destruction
- Ammo: 4

## Long Needler

- 30 Kinetic, 40 Penetration, 2 Destruction, 12% Bleed (x2)
- Aimed Shot: 100% ACC
- Scoped Shot: 190% ACC
- Ammo: 8

## Long Blaster

- 50 Energy, 35 Penetration, 20 Destruction, 80% Fire, 0% Bleed
- Range: 16 (6.25), max 32
- Ammo: 6

## Fusion Disintegrator

- 120 Energy, 30 Penetration, 40 Destruction, 85% Fire, 16% Bleed (x3)
- Range: 16 (5), max 36
- Shot modes return to Xenonaut baseline
- Ammo: 4
